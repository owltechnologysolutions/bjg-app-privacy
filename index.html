BJG App - Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026
Last updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Owl Technology Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the BJG App (the "App") on iOS or Android. By creating an account or otherwise using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who we are and how to contact us

The BJG App is developed and operated by Owl Technology Solutions. If you have any questions, requests, or complaints about this policy or your data, please contact us:

Owl Technology Solutions
Privacy contact: Colin King
Email: colin.king@owltechnologysolutions.org

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.

2. Summary (plain English)

  • We collect the minimum we need to run the App: your email and password (used to sign in), and the golf-related data you create (rounds, scores, courses, etc.).
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase. Your password is hashed; we never see it in plain text.
  • The App collects anonymous usage telemetry to help us improve the product. Telemetry events are tied to a random per-session UUID, not to your identity.
  • We do not show ads. We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs beyond the in-house telemetry described here.
  • The App does not access your location, camera, contacts, microphone, or push notifications.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 9).

3. Data we collect

We use the data categories defined in the Google Play Data Safety taxonomy where applicable.

3.1 Personal info

  • Email address - used as your login identifier and to send essential account-related messages (for example, password reset emails).

3.2 Authentication info

  • Password - stored only in hashed form by our authentication provider, Supabase. Owl Technology Solutions never sees or stores your plain-text password.
  • Session tokens (JWTs) and password-reset tokens - issued by Supabase to keep you signed in and to verify password resets. On your device, the active session is stored in the operating system's secure store (iOS Keychain on Apple devices; Android EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android devices) via Expo SecureStore. We use the PKCE OAuth flow for added security on mobile.

3.3 App activity (golf data you create)

  • Profile information you choose to enter.
  • Golf-related content you create or submit, such as rounds, scores, courses, and related notes.
  • Interactions with the App that produce records (for example, saving a round).

3.4 App performance and product analytics (telemetry)

  • Anonymous-style event logs describing how the App is being used in aggregate (for example, which screens are opened, which features are used, basic error or crash signals).
  • Each telemetry session is tagged with a random session UUID generated on-device using expo-crypto. This UUID is not linked to your account, email, or any other identifier we hold.
  • We use telemetry strictly for product improvement and stability. We do not use it for advertising and we do not share it with advertising networks.

3.5 Financial info (planned, not yet active in v1)

  • If and when paid features are introduced, payments will be processed by Stripe. Stripe collects and processes your card or other payment details directly. Owl Technology Solutions will not receive, see, or store your card number or full payment credentials at any time.
  • From Stripe we will store only: a Stripe customer ID, plus minimal receipt metadata (such as amount, currency, date, and product purchased) so we can show you your purchase history and provide support.
  • This section will be updated and the in-app disclosures refreshed before any paid functionality is enabled.

3.6 Device or other IDs

  • The random per-session telemetry UUID described in 3.4. We do not use the device advertising ID, IDFA, IDFV, Android Advertising ID, or any persistent hardware identifier.

3.7 What we do NOT collect

  • Precise or approximate location.
  • Camera, photo library, or microphone data.
  • Contacts, calendar, or SMS data.
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data.
  • Push notification tokens (the App does not send push notifications in v1).
  • Advertising identifiers.

4. How we use your data

  • To provide the App - authenticate you, save your golf data, and let you read it back from any device where you sign in.
  • To secure your account - issue and verify session tokens, process password resets, and detect abusive activity.
  • To support you - respond to questions or issues you send to our privacy contact.
  • To improve the App - review aggregated, non-identifying telemetry to find bugs and prioritize features.
  • To process payments (when paid features launch) - via Stripe, and to maintain a record of your purchases.
  • To comply with the law - meet our legal obligations and enforce our terms.

5. Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it with advertisers. We share data only with the service providers listed below, and only to the extent needed for them to perform a service for us:

5.1 Supabase (authentication and identity)

Supabase, Inc. (US-hosted) provides our authentication and identity service. Supabase stores your email, hashed password, and the session and password-reset tokens that Supabase issues. See the Supabase Privacy Policy for details about how Supabase processes data.

5.2 Owl Technology Solutions backend (Django API)

Our application backend, operated by Owl Technology Solutions, stores your profile and your golf-related App data (rounds, scores, courses, and similar). Requests between the App and the backend are authenticated using your Supabase-issued JWT and are sent over HTTPS.

5.3 Stripe (planned, not active in v1)

When paid features are introduced, payments will be processed by Stripe, Inc. Stripe is a PCI-DSS Level 1 service provider and handles your full payment credentials directly. See the Stripe Privacy Policy.

5.4 Legal and safety disclosures

We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to comply with a valid legal process (such as a subpoena or court order), to protect the safety of any person, to address fraud or security issues, or to enforce our terms of service.

5.5 No advertising or third-party analytics SDKs

Beyond the providers above, the App does not include any third-party advertising networks, third-party analytics SDKs, attribution SDKs, or data-broker integrations.

6. How we secure your data

  • In transit: all network requests between the App and our backend, between the App and Supabase, and between our backend and Supabase use HTTPS / TLS.
  • At rest: passwords are stored only as salted hashes by Supabase. Account and golf data stored in our backend database is protected by encryption at rest provided by our hosting infrastructure.
  • On your device: active sessions are stored in the operating system's hardware-backed secure store (iOS Keychain or Android EncryptedSharedPreferences) via Expo SecureStore.
  • Access controls: access to production systems by Owl Technology Solutions personnel is limited to the people who need it to operate or support the App.

No method of transmission or storage on the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data retention

  • Account data (email, profile, golf data) is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Authentication and session tokens expire automatically based on Supabase's standard token lifetimes; expired tokens are not retained.
  • Telemetry events (which contain only a random session UUID) are retained for up to 13 months to allow year-over-year product comparison, then deleted or aggregated into non-identifying summaries.
  • Receipt and Stripe customer ID metadata (once paid features launch) will be retained for at least the period required by applicable tax and accounting law (typically up to 7 years in the United States), even if you delete your account.
  • When you request deletion, we will remove your account-level data within 30 days, subject to the legal-retention exception above for financial records.

8. Your choices and rights

You can:

  • Update your profile information from inside the App.
  • Reset your password from the sign-in screen.
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, or request that we correct it.
  • Request that we delete your account and associated personal data (see Section 9).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at colin.king@owltechnologysolutions.org.

9. Account and data deletion

You can request deletion of your BJG App account and the personal data associated with it through any of the following channels:

  • In-app: open the App, go to Settings, and choose "Delete account". This initiates an account-deletion request.
  • Public web request (no login required): visit our account-deletion request page or email the address below from the email you used to register. You do not need an account or to be signed in to make a deletion request. The current public deletion request URL is this Privacy Policy page; submit your request by emailing colin.king@owltechnologysolutions.org with the subject line "BJG App account deletion request" and the email address associated with your account.
  • Email fallback: emailing colin.king@owltechnologysolutions.org with the subject line "BJG App account deletion request" will, on its own, trigger deletion of your account and associated personal data within 30 days of verification.

What gets deleted: your profile, your authentication record at Supabase, the golf-related App data tied to your account, and any telemetry that is tied to your account (note: most telemetry is not tied to your account at all and is already anonymous).

What we may retain after deletion (and why):

  • Aggregated or de-identified analytics that can no longer be linked to you.
  • Records we are legally required to keep, such as financial and tax records associated with completed purchases (typically up to 7 years).
  • Information necessary to detect, investigate, or prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

10. Children's privacy

The BJG App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at colin.king@owltechnologysolutions.org and we will delete that information. This policy is intended to comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

11. International users

The App is operated from the United States, and data we collect is processed and stored on servers located in the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States.

11.1 Notice for users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (or its UK equivalent) gives you certain rights with respect to your personal data.

Legal bases we rely on:

  • Performance of a contract - to create and operate your account, store your golf data, and deliver the App's core functionality.
  • Legitimate interests - to keep the App secure, debug problems, and improve the product through aggregated telemetry. These interests are balanced against your privacy rights.
  • Consent - where required for any optional processing. You can withdraw consent at any time, without affecting processing carried out before withdrawal.
  • Legal obligation - where we must retain or disclose data to comply with the law.

Your rights: access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restriction of processing, objection to processing, and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, email colin.king@owltechnologysolutions.org. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

11.2 Notice for California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act, gives you the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you, where we got it, why we use it, and who we share it with (this Privacy Policy provides that information).
  • Delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions (see Section 9).
  • Correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
  • Opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not sell or share your personal information for these purposes, so there is nothing to opt out of.
  • Non-discrimination - we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

To make a CCPA / CPRA request, email colin.king@owltechnologysolutions.org. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling the request.
